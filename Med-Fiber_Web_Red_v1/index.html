Med-Fiber LLC

Mid Infrared Optical Fibers for Laser Applications

 

Med-Fiber LLC is a leading supplier of infrared transmitting fiber optics for laser power delivery applications.

Company Profile

Med-Fiber LLC produces Germanium Oxide glass fiber (HP fiber) for use with the Er:YAG and Er:YSGG lasers. These lasers coupled with HP fiber delivery systems can be used for painless dentistry and precise ablation without thermal damage in laser surgery procedures.
