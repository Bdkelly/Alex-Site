A key component to the Er:YAG, Er:YSGG or Ho:YAG mid-infrared laser system is the optical fiber, which is used to transmit the laser power from the laser to the patient. HP Fiber can handle up to 20 Watts of laser power for applications in dermatology, dentistry, ophthalmology, orthopedics and general surgery.
